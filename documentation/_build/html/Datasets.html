Data sets¶

Olympic Peninsula project¶

The price-response of a group of flexible consumers that participated in the Olympic Peninsula project can be found in [R1].

Pacific Northwest Smart Grid demonstration project¶

A large amount of data from the Pacific Northwest Smart Grid demonstration project can be found in [R2].

Low Carbon London project¶

Energy consumption readings for a sample of 5,567 London Households that took part in the UK Power Networks led Low Carbon London project between November 2011 and February 2014. For more information, the reader is referred to [R3].
