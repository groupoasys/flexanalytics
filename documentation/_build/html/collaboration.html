Collaboration with Other Groups¶

The project Flexanalytics has also produced the following research results in collaboration with other international research groups:

  1. D. Guericke, I. Blanco, J. M. Morales and H. Madsen , A two-phase stochastic programming approach to biomass supply planning for combined heat and power plants, OR Spectrum, DOI: 10.1007/s00291-020-00593-x, June 2020.
  2. G. De Zotti, S. A. Pourmousavi, J. M. Morales, H. Madsen and N. K. Poulsen, A Control-based Method to Meet TSO and DSO Ancillary Services Needs by Flexible End-users, IEEE Transactions on Power Systems vol. 35, no. 3, pp. 1868–1880, May 2020.
  3. N. Mazzi, A. Trivella, and J. M. Morales, Enabling Active/Passive Electricity Trading in Dual-Price Balancing Markets, IEEE Transactions on Power Systems, vol. 34, no. 3, pp. 1980–1990, May 2019.
  4. G. De Zotti, S. A. Pourmousavi, J. M. Morales, H. Madsen, and N. K. Poulsen, Consumers’ Flexibility Estimation at the TSO Level for Balancing Services, IEEE Transactions on Power Systems, vol. 34, no. 3, pp. 1918–1930, May 2019.
  5. V. Dvorkin, S. Delikaraoglou and J. M. Morales, Setting Reserve Requirements to Approximate the Efficiency of the Stochastic Dispatch, IEEE Transactions on Power Systems, vol. 34, no. 2, pp. 1524–1536, Mar. 2019.
  6. C. Ordoudis, P. Pinson, and J. M. Morales, An Integrated Market for Electricity and Natural Gas Systems with Stochastic Power Producers, European Journal of Operational Research, vol. 272, no. 2, pp. 642–654, Jan. 2019.
